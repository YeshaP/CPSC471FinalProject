About Us

Founded in Calgary, Canada, Mbiant Clothing and Shoes is a women's and men's apparel company. Mbiant beleives in high-quality, comfortbale, athetlic clothing ranging from t-shirts, outerwear, and shoes. With over 50 stores across Canada and locations in over 40 countries Mbiant is one of the fastest growing women's and men's athletic fashion brands. Mbiant's main goal is to inspire healthy living for all across the globe all while being trendy and comfy!
